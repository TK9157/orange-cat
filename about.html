📖 The Legend & The Mission 📖

🐱 The Orange Gene

Approximately 80% of orange cats are male. The gene responsible for their distinct color is located on the X chromosome. This genetic uniqueness comes with a personality that is famously vibrant, friendly, and extremely chatty.

🧠 The Single Braincell

In internet lore, it is believed that all orange cats share a single brain cell. When one gets it, they display absolute genius, but most of the time they operate on pure, delightful, zero-braincell chaos.

📸 The Gallery Goal

Our project celebrates the unpredictable spirit of orange cats. By compiling a gallery of their daily moments, chaotic play, and cute naps, we bring warmth and laughter to orange cat lovers worldwide.
